Important:

This website provides general, educational nutrition information only. It does not diagnose or treat conditions. Please consult a qualified healthcare professional for personalized advice.

How this works

Choose a nutrient and (optionally) your age and diet style. The site will show common foods that are good sources of the chosen nutrient and a gentle sample day-of-meals that emphasize those foods. All suggestions are educational.

Quick tips

  • Prefer whole foods (fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, dairy or alternatives).
  • Combine iron-rich plant foods with vitamin C sources to help absorption (educational tip).
  • Variety across food groups helps meet many nutrient needs.
